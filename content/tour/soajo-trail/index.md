## Experience the Magic of Soajo

Join us on this unique adventure through Peneda Gerês National Park, where we'll explore the most beautiful trails in the Soajo region. This guided walk offers a perfect opportunity to connect with nature, learn about the rich local flora and fauna, and discover the secrets of this protected natural area.

During this hike, we'll visit iconic locations such as hidden waterfalls, traditional watermills, and the breathtaking landscapes that make this region so special.
